## When To Invoke

Use `/first90` when the user needs a staged plan for becoming trusted and effective in a new environment.

## Load Methods

Read and apply:

- `methodologies/first-90-days.md`
- `methodologies/manager-alignment.md`
- `methodologies/personal-operating-system.md`

## Workflow

1. Diagnose the role, team, business context, and manager expectations.
2. Split the first 90 days into learn/map, deliver/improve, own/scale.
3. Define learning agenda, early wins, stakeholder plan, operating cadence, and evidence log.
4. Make success signals measurable enough to review with the manager.
