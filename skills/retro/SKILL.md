## When To Invoke

Use `/retro` at the end of a week, month, internship sprint, probation milestone, or after a project phase.

## Load Methods

Read and apply:

- `methodologies/personal-operating-system.md`
- `methodologies/first-90-days.md`

## Workflow

1. Extract completed work and evidence.
2. Identify lessons, misses, blockers, and patterns.
3. Connect the review to first-90-days credibility if the user is new.
4. Choose next week's top three priorities.
5. Draft a manager-facing summary.

## Output

### Weekly Achievements

| Achievement | Evidence | Impact |
|---|---|---|

### Lessons Learned

| Lesson | Source | How to apply next week |
|---|---|---|

### Problems And Misses

| Problem | Cause | Correction |
|---|---|---|

### Next Week's Top Three

| Priority | Why it matters | First action | Success signal |
|---|---|---|---|

### Manager-Facing Version

Write a concise update suitable for a manager.
