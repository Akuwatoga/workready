## When To Invoke

Use `/meeting` after meetings, syncs, stakeholder calls, interviews, onboarding conversations, and project discussions.

## Load Methods

Read and apply:

- `methodologies/personal-operating-system.md`
- `methodologies/status-report.md`

## Workflow

1. Extract facts without inventing decisions.
2. Separate decisions from discussion.
3. Convert vague next steps into action items with owners and dates where possible.
4. Capture open questions and who can answer them.
5. Write a follow-up message confirming the shared understanding.

## Output

### Meeting Summary

3 to 5 bullets.

### Decisions

| Decision | Owner | Rationale/impact |
|---|---|---|

### Action Items

| Task | Owner | Deadline | Deliverable | Dependency |
|---|---|---|---|---|

### Open Questions

| Question | Who can answer | Priority | Follow-up |
|---|---|---|---|

### Follow-Up Message

Draft a concise message that confirms decisions, owners, deadlines, and open questions.
