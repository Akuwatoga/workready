## When To Invoke

Use `/datamap` when the user needs to understand workflows, spreadsheets, dashboards, reports, CRMs, forms, data ownership, or AI agent opportunities.

## Load Methods

Read and apply:

- `methodologies/business-data-map.md`
- `methodologies/personal-operating-system.md`

## Workflow

1. Map the business workflow before proposing automation.
2. Identify data sources, owners, key fields, access paths, and quality issues.
3. Link reports or dashboards to their upstream sources.
4. Identify automation opportunities by value, frequency, risk reduction, and difficulty.
5. Produce a practical backlog with human approval boundaries.
