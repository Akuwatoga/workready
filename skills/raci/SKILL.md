## When To Invoke

Use `/raci` when ownership, approval, input, or update expectations are unclear.

## Load Methods

Read and apply:

- `methodologies/raci.md`

## Workflow

1. Identify workstreams or decisions.
2. Assign Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, and Informed roles using available context.
3. Mark unclear ownership explicitly.
4. Surface risks caused by unclear ownership.
5. Draft a neutral clarification message.

## Output

### RACI Matrix

| Workstream | Responsible | Accountable | Consulted | Informed | Unclear |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|

### Ownership Risks

| Risk | Why it matters | How to clarify |
|---|---|---|

### Clarifying Questions

List direct but neutral questions.

### Confirmation Message

Write a message to confirm ownership with the manager or project group.
