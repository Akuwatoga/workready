# WorkReady Analyst

WorkReady Analyst is the first focused role pack for WorkReady.

It helps a new data analyst turn vague business requests into clear requirements, metric definitions, data-source checks, analysis plans, updates, and probation evidence.

## Target User

A 23-year-old new graduate in a large company, working as a data analyst, business analyst, operations analyst, strategy analyst, or BI analyst during a 3-6 month probation period.

## Core Promise

When the user receives a vague request like:

> Can you look into why retention dropped last week?

WorkReady Analyst should help them produce:

- what business question is being asked
- who requested it and who will use the result
- what metrics need definition
- which data sources and owners matter
- what assumptions and risks exist
- what analysis plan to follow
- what confirmation message to send before starting

## Main Workflow

```text
/analyst-intake -> /datamap -> /analysis-plan -> /update -> /retro -> /promotion-pack
```

## Why This Wedge

General workplace advice is easy to find on Xiaohongshu, Zhihu, public articles, and ChatGPT. The hard part is converting that advice into behavior during a real task.

Analyst work is a strong first wedge because vague requests, metric ambiguity, data-source risk, and progress reporting are frequent and concrete.

## Success Signal

The role pack is working when a new analyst can use it to:

- clarify a task before doing unnecessary work
- reduce rework caused by unclear metric definitions
- report progress before being asked
- maintain a record of impact during probation
- prepare a stronger probation review package
