# RACI Method

Use this method when work involves multiple people and ownership is unclear.

## Definitions

| Role | Meaning | Test |
|---|---|---|
| Responsible | Does the work | Who is executing the task? |
| Accountable | Owns the outcome or final decision | Who is answerable if this succeeds or fails? |
| Consulted | Gives input before the decision or work is finalized | Whose expertise or approval context is needed? |
| Informed | Receives updates after progress or decisions | Who needs visibility but not active input? |

## Operating Rules

- Every workstream should have at least one Responsible owner.
- Every workstream should have exactly one Accountable owner where possible.
- Too many Consulted people slow decisions; include only people whose input changes the work.
- Informed people should receive concise updates, not meeting invitations by default.
- If Accountable is unclear, escalate as an ownership question before doing deep work.

## Output Pattern

| Workstream | Responsible | Accountable | Consulted | Informed | Decision needed |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|

## Clarifying Question Pattern

```text
To avoid duplicated work, can we confirm ownership for this workstream?

My current understanding:
- Responsible:
- Accountable:
- Consulted:
- Informed:

The unclear part is:
```
